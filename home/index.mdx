## Welcome to NewsCatcher

NewsCatcher provides enterprise-grade APIs for accessing news data, searching the web for real-world events, and discovering hyper-local news. Whether you're building news aggregators, conducting market research, developing AI agents, or training large language models, our APIs deliver structured, enriched data from over 120,000 sources worldwide.
